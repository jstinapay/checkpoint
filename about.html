About checkpoint.

Checkpoint is the definitive platform for gamers, bridging the gap between tracking and community discussion. While current platforms force users to choose between maintaining a personal library and engaging in deep discussion, Checkpoint integrates them into a seamless ecosystem. We, as the developers, will design it to be some sort of hub for your gaming experience. A hub where your game progress unlocks relevant discussions and your reviews spark community debates rather than shouting into the void.
